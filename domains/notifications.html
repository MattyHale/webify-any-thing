Notifications and Event Triggers

Notifications connect system state changes to action.

What is Expected

The platform must notify users and systems when important events occur, such as data availability or job completion. Support email, in-app alerts, SMS, and machine-readable callbacks like webhooks.

Why It’s Important

Timely alerts enable faster response and automation. They close the loop from capture to action without polling.

Key Notification Principles

Real-Time Alerts

Emit notifications quickly with sufficient context for action.

User-Defined Subscriptions

Allow users to define filters, channels, and cadence.

Integration with External Systems

Provide reliable webhooks and messaging options for downstream automation.

Reliable Delivery

Implement retries, delivery logs, batching, and rate limits.

Operational Communications

Maintain a visible status channel for maintenance and incidents.
